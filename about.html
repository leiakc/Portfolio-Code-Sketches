Leia KC is a multidisciplinary artist who is currently exploring her creative practice while attending OCAD University’s Digital Futures program.

Previously, Leia sold her handmade jewellery at the OCAD Artist Alley, worked as a product photographer for Trudelle Laker Jewellery, participated in a panel discussion with FeelSpace Curatorial about art in digital space, and exhibited her artwork at Lumenus Youth Gallery.
